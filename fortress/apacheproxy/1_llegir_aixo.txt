INSTALL APACHE2
===============

(0) Editar fitxer .\conf\httpd.conf i definir la variable SRVROOT que apunti a un directori correcte
(1) Obrir consola com administrador i anar a .\bin
(2) Executar "httpd -k install"


START
=====

Executar "httpd -k start"



HTTPS: GEN CERTIFICATS
======================

(a) Anar a directori /conf
(b) Executar 'openssl req -new -newkey rsa:4096 -x509 -sha256 -days 365 -nodes -out server.crt -keyout server.key'
